01

What is Broker Whiteboard?

Sphere Broker Whiteboard is a collaborative, real-time pricing screen for broking desks. Every member of your desk works from the same shared live board simultaneously — prices entered by any one person are immediately visible to the whole team, keeping the desk aligned without any manual coordination.


The board can be populated from multiple sources: manual entry, imported broker chat messages processed instantly by AI, or dictated via voice. The platform also directly integrates a WhatsApp Blaster enabling price distribution to dozens of clients in seconds from the same interface.

Price Colour Guide

Every price on the board is colour coded to immediately communicate its source, recency, and tradeability. Understanding this colour system is the foundation of reading the board at a glance.

Green
Live price

Posted directly onto the board by a member of your desk. Fixed until manually removed or paused. This may be used to indicate the firm prices you are working.

Live
Yellow
Indicative price

A price posted as indicative rather than live. Yellow prices fade over time, reflecting their recency.

Indicative
Blue
Implied price — live

Generated automatically by the platform from the constituent legs of other prices on the board. A blue implied price is composed entirely of live (green) prices. Hover over the price to see the breakdown of its constituent legs.

Implied — Live
Pink
Implied price — indicative

An implied price where at least one constituent leg is indicative (yellow). Hover over the price to see the breakdown of its constituent legs.

Implied — Indicative
Grey
Private price

An internal private price used for reference purposes.

Private
04

Populating the Screen

The board can be populated in three ways.

Manual Entry

Click the add price icon or click directly on a cell in the grid. The instrument and expiry will be pre-filled. Complete the remaining fields and submit.

Import Chat Prices

Paste an ICE or WhatsApp message directly into the import field. Cognitive AI extracts instrument, expiry, direction, quantity, and counterparty automatically.

Voice Widget

Dictate prices using the shorthand your desk already uses. The voice widget parses speech and posts the price to the grid immediately without a confirmation step.

05

Logging Deals

When a price on the board has been successfully dealt, rather than simply removing it you can book the deal directly on the grid.

1
Right-click the dealt price on the grid

A context menu will appear with the option to select Deal.

2
Select Deal

This registers that the price was transacted by your team. The price is removed from the live grid.

3
Deal appears in the Deals Panel and Activity Ticker

Both panels update immediately, creating a timestamped, attributed record of the transaction. The Deals Panel is filterable back over the prior two weeks.

Deals can also be input via voice.

The Chat Stream

The chat stream sits in the lower portion of the left panel and displays a real-time feed of inbound communications extracted from incoming WhatsApp messages. Rather than monitoring dozens of individual conversations, all relevant price data surfaces in one consolidated place.

Clicking any entry in the chat stream takes you directly into that conversation in the WhatsApp panel above, allowing you to respond or take action immediately.

Pricing information extracted from incoming WhatsApp messages appears here in real time, regardless of which conversation it came from.

Clicking an entry opens that conversation in the WhatsApp panel above, where you can respond or take action immediately.

Benefit

During periods of high market activity, the chat stream ensures no inbound communication is missed across your broker network, without needing to open each conversation individually.
